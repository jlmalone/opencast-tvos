FCast Receiver for Apple TV

Privacy Policy & App Information

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

FCast Receiver for Apple TV does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever.

What this app does

FCast Receiver listens on your local network for incoming media streams from FCast-compatible sender apps and plays them through your Apple TV. All communication happens entirely on your local network between your devices.

Data we collect

None. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in required
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No usage tracking
  • No data is sent to any server — by us or any third party

Local network access

The app uses your local network exclusively to receive media streams from sender devices on the same network. It advertises itself via Bonjour/mDNS so sender apps can discover it automatically. No data leaves your local network.

Third-party services

This app has no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or external services. It is built entirely on Apple's native frameworks (SwiftUI, AVFoundation, Network.framework).

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data from anyone, there are no special considerations for children.

Changes to this policy

If anything changes, this page will be updated. The app collects no data and this is unlikely to change.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/jlmalone/fcast-appletv.

About FCast

FCast is an open casting protocol designed by FUTO. This app is an independent community implementation and is not affiliated with or endorsed by FUTO.
